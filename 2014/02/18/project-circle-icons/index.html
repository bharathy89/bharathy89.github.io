Project - Circle Icons!

I had a good day today! I got my hands dirty doing vector art its fun and I love it. These are some of the icons I could make really fast. Its so easy if you are using the right tools. I am using a trial version of sketch app for mac. I generally look for open source alternatives but I thought Ill give this a try and its addictive.

I like flat colors 2013 has been the year of flat colors. I hope this trend doesnt go away for some time because its kind of easy for me to draw simple shapes and dont have to spend much time on textures and shadows and other stuff. :D

I got my inspiration from circle icons they obviously are much prettier than mine. Baby steps!!
